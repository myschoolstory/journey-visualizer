The Closed-Loop Advantage

Unlike fossil fuels, which release carbon trapped underground for millions of years, sugarcane ethanol is part of a **short-cycle biogenic loop**.

The Energy Balance

Sugarcane bioethanol offers an energy balance of up to 9.2, meaning for every 1 unit of fossil energy used in production, 9.2 units of renewable energy are delivered. This is significantly higher than corn ethanol (approx. 1.5) or cellulosic ethanol (approx. 4-5).
